About Us

IT Study Lab was founded in 2022 by a group of network engineers and cybersecurity enthusiasts. We noticed a lack of simple, no-frills educational content for core IT concepts. Our goal is to maintain a small, curated library of resources to help students pass their certification exams and build confidence in their technical skills.
